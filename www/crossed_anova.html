+
|

εijk is the error term, which represents the amount by which an observed variate differs from the value predicted by the model.

,
|

εijk∼N(0, σ2) represents two of the basic ANOVA error assumptions:
1) Errors are normally distributed with a zero mean.
2) Error variance is constant across treatments.
The additional error assumption is that they are all independent.
